AVP GLOBAL SCHOOL

School Level

Pre Primary

Early childhood and preschool education is a phase of introducing children to fundamental learning through the use of a medium that is engaging , less straining and more recreational in nature. We at AVP Global School Pre Primary recognize this need and realize that the child`s time spent with us is important to initiate his holistic growth. The Pre Primary Curriculum is designed for three years of preschool, Nursery, Jr.KG and Sr.KG

Primary

AVP Global School is not just a school, but a plethora of education. We have classes right from Grade I to Grade V

Middle

Middle School is an educational state which exists to provide education between Primary level and Secondary level. The academic year commences from April and ends in March. It consists of a curriculum of Scholastic and Co-Scholastic Activities. Keeping in mind that the students need to grow more independent during this period, we make a conscious effort to expose students to a plethora of activities.

Curriculum

In the light of emerging needs and new development in school education, the AVP curriculum is holistic, developmentally appropriate, indigenous and most important play and activity based.

Pre Primary

Alphabets (Aa-Zz) are introduced for recognition of letters and their correct pronunciation -Phonetically. In class KG, the children are introduced to patterns, writing letters , two and three letter words , paragraphs and written representation.

Develop a child`s ability to recognize basic numbers from 0-10 and various mathematical concepts. In KG numbers are added (1-100) the concepts of big/small, tall/short etc. With a clear perceptive of shapes is taken up. Additional comparative concepts are taken up on higher level.

In Sr.KG Hindi language is introduced with the help picture recognition Swar and Vyanjans.

Awareness about environmental and child`s perspective towards Self, School, Family, Air, Water, Manners, Birds, Animals, Nature and Surroundings, People Around Us, Seasons, Transport etc.

Primary

Language enhancement sessions for extempore, conversation, listening skills etc. are a regular part of curriculum for students where the grammar rules and vocabulary related to a particular topic is introduced.

Knowledge or skill that someone acquires through experiential learning rather than just reading or observing will create a lasting impact, so the lessons are planned with a lot of activities and projects.

Project based learning and math fun activities are incorporated in order to develop problem solving skills, understand abstract concepts, apply knowledge in their day to day activities.

Awareness about environmental and child`s perspective towards Self, School, Family, Air, Water, Manners, Birds, Animals, Nature and Surroundings, People Around Us, Seasons, Transport etc.

Middle

Person Aster is a comprehensive course for the teaching and learning of English for Grades VI - VIII. The course aims to develop communication skills in students by integrating the essential language skills of listening, speaking, reading and writing.

Udbhay course books are an excellent source to develop comprehension and reading skills in Hindi language. The workbook is a follow-up of the book to reinforce the learning of the students.

In addition to English and Hindi ,Grade VI students are introduced to a third language "Sanskrit"
